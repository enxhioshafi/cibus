Maria Smith

Co-fouder, chef

Maria Smith is a founder of Cibus and sits on two all-party Parliamentary Groups – A Fit and Healthy Childhood and Healthy Eating In Schools. She has contributed to two major papers covering the need of healthy eating in schools and children generally.
